Welcome to I.P.C Zion Church at Leipzig

“Those who trust in the Lord are like Mount Zion, which cannot be shaken but endures forever.” — Psalm 125:1

At Zion I.P.C, we believe in being set apart—just as Mount Zion stands immovable, so too does the dwelling place of God remain steadfast and holy. God does not dwell in just any place; His presence fills a sanctuary that is distinct from the world, where His grace shines brightly.

We are a Christ-centered community devoted to true worship. But what is true worship? It goes beyond singing songs or clapping hands on a Sunday. True worship is the daily offering of our lives—presenting ourselves as living sacrifices, holy and pleasing to God.

With this heart and vision, we have begun a new chapter by planting Zion I.P.C in Leipzig. Our mission is simple yet profound: to proclaim the Good News of the crucified and risen Christ, to be a light in this city, and to build a community rooted in faith, love, and truth.

This is just the beginning. We believe greater things are yet to come!

What is Church?

Church is not a building—it’s a living, breathing community of believers. A family bonded by faith, grounded in grace, and driven by love.

“Now you are the body of Christ, and each one of you is a part of it.”
— 1 Corinthians 12:27

The Purpose of the Church

The Church glorifies God, proclaims salvation, and nurtures believers. It’s a place of hope and transformation.

“Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation.”
— Mark 16:15

The Ministries of the Church

Through ministries that care for the needy and support families, the Church shares God’s love beyond its walls.

“Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others...”
— 1 Peter 4:10

The Power of Fellowship

Fellowship strengthens faith, fosters unity, and reflects Christ through shared worship and prayer.

“For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them.”
— Matthew 18:20
